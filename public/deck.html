The Problem

No tool exists for OTG players
to understand what they own

  • Items scattered across Xbox, PlayStation, Epic, Steam — all decoupled
  • No cost basis tracking — no record of what you paid
  • No comparable sales data across markets
  • Players settle for Discord price checks and rough estimates
  • GUN token fluctuations make USD P&L impossible to reason about
Validated By
  • OTG Discord member publicly requested an inventory value tool
  • Gunzilla team member tagged the builder to make it happen
  • No competing tool existed — confirmed by community and team
